Welcome to Skate or Dice!

Skate or dice is a special game dedicated just for skateboarding. Each player rolls the dice, if they don't land the trick they get a letter. When they have spelt S.K.A.T.E. they are out. The last one standing is the winner.

Click each button once to see what you get. And may god be in your soles!
